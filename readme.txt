== Description ==
When activated, this plugin will help you to save your post and website from the spammers by removing the **website/URL** input field from comment section of your posts.  It's very important to be safe from the spammers because they write illegal comments and post their website link to get backlinks which can effect your post SEO and performance.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Do I need to make any settings for the plugin to work ?=
No. No additional settings required. Simply Installl and activate the plugin to get the website input field removed.

= Why remove website field ?=
Many spammers use comment sections to promote their sites. They always leave a backlink to a site. Those links can be of any type of sites and may not be good for your website.
The perfect way to discourage spam is to remove the *Website/URL* field entirely from your WordPress website.
